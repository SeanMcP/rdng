<CVQ>

Search for English words that follow a particular consonant-vowel pattern.
**Uppercase C or V letters** match any consonant or vowel, respectively, while
**lowercase letters** match exact letters.

- **CVC** will match words with any consonant, any vowel, and any consonant:
  bat, cot, dog, fig, _etc_.
- **CVCe** will match word with any consonant, vowel, consonant, and a final
  "e": byte, cake, dome, fine, _etc_.

</CVQ>
